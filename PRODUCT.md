# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Two roles share the surface. The **owner** (the station director) programs the single channel: schedules YouTube videos and self-hosted uploads into a running programme. **Viewers** are friends, family, and followers who open Station One to lean back and watch whatever is on air, on phone, tablet, laptop, or TV. The Phase 0 prototype is built for viewers reached by a shareable web link, with the owner validating demand; it is designed web-first but mobile-ready from the start.

## Product Purpose

Station One is a personal TV station: one owner-programmed channel, always playing, mixing YouTube embeds and self-hosted video, that learns each viewer's taste over time. The Phase 0 prototype exists to validate demand cheaply: a working web player with one channel, an auto-advancing programme, and Love/Skip signals, shareable with test viewers before any native app is built.

## Positioning

The only TV station programmed by a person, not a platform — with its own app, one channel, and a programme that tunes itself to you.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic premium. Dark, confident, quiet: the feel of a home theater, not a content feed. Emotional goals: the calm relief of switching on a TV that already knows what to play, and pride of ownership for the station director. The interface stays out of the way; the programme is the show.

## Anti-references

- Retro-TV kitsch (test cards, CRT static, VHS texture) — considered and rejected in favor of cinematic premium.
- A YouTube clone shell: thumbnails-and-feed browsing, recommendation grids, engagement bait.
- Corporate FAST-platform density (Pluto-style wall-of-channels EPG grids); Station One has one channel and no browsing paralysis.

## Design Principles

1. **Lean-back first.** The programme is always playing; every UI element must justify interrupting it.
2. **The station has an owner.** Human curation is visible — "programmed by" is a feature, not a footnote.
3. **The player is sacred.** Nothing ever covers the video surface (also a YouTube ToS requirement); all chrome lives beside or below it.
4. **One channel, one truth.** The guide shows now and next, never a catalog.
5. **Compliance is design.** Source badges, ungated playback, and intact YouTube ads are built into the visual system, not patched on.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: 4.5:1 minimum text contrast on the dark theme, fully keyboard-operable player and guide, visible focus states, `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for every animation, and caption support respected wherever players provide it.
