Station One

Programmed by a person. Tuned to you.

The brand guide for Station One: a personal TV station with one channel, always on. The personality is cinematic premium: dark, confident, quiet. The interface is the dark room; the programme is the show.

Identity

The mark is a screen holding a play signal, always in Signal Red on black. The pulsing on-air dot is the signature: it means the station is live, and it appears in exactly two places (nav and on-air badge), nowhere else.

Color

Restrained: OLED black and near-black neutrals carry everything; Signal Red stays under 10% of any screen and always means something (on air, primary action, current selection). The video is the color; the interface is the room around it.

Typography

Two families with strict jobs. Instrument Sans does all the work: labels, buttons, body, data, on a fixed 1.2 scale. Bricolage Grotesque is the station's voice, reserved for the wordmark, one screen title at most, and the off-air card.

Voice

The app talks like a station, quietly. Broadcast words, verbs for buttons, no hype.

Say

  • On air · Up next · Later tonight · Off air
  • Tune innot "sign in to watch"
  • Programmenot "queue" or "playlist"
  • Love · Skip · Start overbuttons are verbs

Never

  • "Content"it's a programme, made by a person
  • "Algorithm" · "For you feed"say "tuned to you"
  • Exclamation marksthe station is calm
  • Engagement baitno streaks, no autoplay nags

Core controls

One primary action per screen, ghost buttons for the rest, and press feedback within 100ms. Every control ships default, hover, focus, active, and disabled states.

Motion: 150–250ms ease-out, state changes only. The one indulgence is the 400ms TV-wake on tune-in; instant cut under reduced motion.

Principles

1

Lean-back first

The programme is always playing. Every UI element must justify interrupting it.

2

The station has an owner

Human curation is visible. "Programmed by Dex" is a feature, not a footnote.

3

The player is sacred

Nothing ever covers the video surface. All chrome lives beside or below the stage.

4

One channel, one truth

The guide shows now and next, never a catalog. No browsing paralysis.

5

Compliance is design

Source badges, ungated playback, and intact YouTube ads are part of the visual system.
